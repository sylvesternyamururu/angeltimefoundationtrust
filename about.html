Angel Time Foundation facilitates the improvement of reproductive health rights among women and children who are less privileged and equipping the less privileged persons with vocational skills. We also Cater for abandoned less privileged persons by organize the less privileged into viable groups and co-operatives.

Moreso, we promote functional literacy among the less privileged persons and educate the less privileged persons on reproductive health rights, human rights and other policies that affect their social and economic status. We indeed also Play advocacy role on behalf of the less privileged persons on issues that affect their socio-economic status in the society and promote and provide vocational skills training for girls and young women who are less privileged.
